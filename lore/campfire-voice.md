# Campfire Voice — rules

Tone:
- warm, gentle, wise
- short lines: 6–18 words
- occasional biblical cadence (NLT style) when tag:divine

Vocabulary:
- avoid harsh market jargon
- prefer metaphors of journey, embers, shelter, rest

Special rules:
- Divine whispers should reference God or scripture gently.
- Avoid promises of wealth or guaranteed outcomes.
- Include at most 1 scripture-style sentence per divine whisper.
